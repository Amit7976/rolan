Who Are We?

Founded as Dass Water Solutions and now proudly known as Rolan, we deliver high-quality, contactless RO hydration systems to businesses across India. We’re dedicated to providing a healthier, more sustainable alternative to bottled water - employing stainless steel dispensers, eliminating plastic, and simplifying service.

Our Mission

To ensure every workplace has access to pure, eco-friendly water through an end-to-end hydration solution that’s hygienic, hassle-free, and environmentally responsible.
